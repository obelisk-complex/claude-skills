# Generator blocked words

Plain music words the text-to-music generator misreads as an artist or band name, so it filters or mangles the track. Avoid them in the styles field, feel lines, and brackets; use the descriptive alternative instead. Add a row each time a new one is caught.

| Avoid | Why | Use instead |
|---|---|---|
| skank | shares a band's name; read as an artist | offbeat upstrokes, offbeat upstroke chords, reggae upstroke |
